Snacks

Candy

Flavored sugar. Absolutely wonderful. Pairs well with soda for a fast track to rotted teeth. Enough said.

Some of our favorite candies are:

  • Airheads
  • Reese's Peanut Butter Cups
  • Werther's Original Hard Candies
  • Those gross Circus Peanuts

Chips

Chips are just the greatest. So much salt and flavoring you'll be hardening your arteries in no time.

Many of the greatest chips include:

  • Doritos
  • Lay's Classic Potato Chips
  • Funyuns
  • Weird shrimp chips from the Japanese grocery store

Pastries

Want something terrible that looks like it might possibly be not as bad for you as candy? Well pastries are your snack. Put a little icing on top and you'll be just fine.

If you aren't eating these, you aren't eating pastries:

  • Donuts
  • Danishes
  • Apple Strudel
  • Croquembouche

Did you think we forgot about cakes?

We couldn't possibly forget about cakes. There are so many varieties and they're just the most wonderful thing you'll ever eat. Yum.
